Our Story

What began as a passion for homemade baking has grown into a beloved home business sharing our treats with the community.

From Our Family to Yours

We started small, baking brownies and blondies for friends and family. The rave reviews encouraged us to share our creations with more people.

Our signature muttai mittai quickly became a customer favorite, with its perfect balance of sweetness and texture. Every batch is made with the same care we'd use for our own family.

We're excited to announce we'll soon be expanding our menu to include delicious veg corn cheese and chicken pizzas, made with the same homemade quality you've come to expect.
